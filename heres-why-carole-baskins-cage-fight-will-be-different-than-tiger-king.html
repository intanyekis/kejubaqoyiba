Here's Why 'Carole Baskin's Cage Fight' Will Be Different Than 'Tiger King'

 Posted on June 5, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  941 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The Tiger King wasn't just a show, it was a modern day phenomenon that quickly became an addiction to millions of viewers across the globe. Everyone tuned in to see what the dramatic big-cat story was all about, and it seemed that no matter how crazy the story surrounding the characters seemed to be, things continued to get more and more twisted with every turn.

During the show, Carole Baskin left a lasting impression on fans, which has opened the door for her new spinoff show called Carole Baskin's Cage Fight. Set to premiere in just a couple short weeks, this docuseries promises to give fans a very different experienced than that of The Tiger King, albeit with the same focus being put on the welfare of these stunning wild animals.

8 It's All About Her Side Of The Story

The South African reveals that one of the major differences between The Tiger King and Carole Baskin's Cage Fight, lies in the fact that this story is completely delivered from Baskin's perspective. It tells the tale of her mission and her work based on what Carole's views and perspectives are, and fans really get to see the inner workings or her life. The messaging that's relayed comes strictly from the standpoint of her personal experience, and fans get to adventure alongside Carole as she faces a series of challenges and contentious situations.

7 It's Her Forum For Animal Rights Activism

Given the fact that this is the new voice for Carole Baskin's tale, fans can expect that her new spinoff show will be used as a forum for her animal rights activism. She will use her ability to reach millions of viewers to the fullest by continuing to aggressively advocate for the fair treatment of animals and for animal rights in every capacity. The welfare and well-being of the animals in her care, and the ones she attempts to rescue, will all be learning examples towards the bigger picture of caring for all living species.

6 She Dives Into Investigating Joe Exotic's Zoo

The Tiger King looked at all angles of the situations that were presented to the audience, but Carole Baskin's Cage Fight promises to isolate Baskins' perspective, thus taking a deep dive into investigating Joe Exotic's Zoo. No stone is left unturned, as Baskin attempts to uncover all the secrets and wrongdoings that were overlooked with Joe Exotic had full control and authority over his property.

5 'Carole Baskin's Cage Fight' Is Behind-The-Scenes And Unfiltered

This new docu-series promises to take fans behind the scenes for an unfiltered, unadapted, and very candid experience. The emotions are raw, the drama is real, and there is nothing scripted for the pleasure of entertainment. The entertainment value comes from the real-life adrenalin that is pumped through to the televisions of millions of viewers across the globe. Carole dives way below the surface of current issues at Joe Exotic's Zoo, and in situations where animals are being ill-treated or uncared for.

4 Viewers Get To Enter A Secret, Dangerous World

There world of big cat rescue is a dark, dangerous, underground world, and Carole Baskin just became a tour guide. She has agreed to reveal many of the deeply embedded secrets surrounding businesses that house large cats, various rescue organization, and those who are willing to do whatever it takes to make money. Divulging these secrets comes at a high price, and Baskin is willing to take the gamble, even if that means exposing the lives of those harming innocent animals in order to do so.

3 Unexpected Alliances

The 13th of November will be a very big day on Discovery, as Carole Baskin joins forces with some very interesting people, to help uncover any secrets that Joe Exotic's old zoo may hold. In a wild twist, she teams up with Joe Exotic's niece, Chealsi Putman, who is said to hold many behind-the-scenes details about the zoo that will prove to be helpful to Baskin's discovery. Another alliance Baskin relies on happens to be a retired homicide detective by the name of Griff Garrison, who lends his extensive experience to the investigation of potential animal abuse and wrongdoing.

2 This Show Promises Confrontations And Drama

Yahoo reports on the introduction of a new side of Carole Baskin that fans have truly never seen before. She and her team of allies stand strong amid danger as they face-off against people from some very shady places. Those accused of heinous crimes against animals go head-to-head against Baskin, as she challenges their livelihood by exposing their unethical tactics. The show packs a lot of drama as both sides firmly stand their ground and the conflicts heat up.

1 It's Poised To Show Carole Baskin In A Different Light

Carole Baskin was portrayed a certain way in The Tiger King, and she refuses to only be seen in one light. The groundbreaking release of this docuseries puts her in full control of Joe Exotic's zoo, and as she reigns supreme with this leverage, she calls the shots and proves that she has sheer will and determination to see her assignment through. With renewed tenacity and far more grit and ferociousness than fans have ever seen before, Carole Baskin leads the way in taking down the "bad guys" to see her animal rights mission through right to the end.
