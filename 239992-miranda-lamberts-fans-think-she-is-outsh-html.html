Miranda Lambert's Fans Think She Is Outshined by Her Gorgeous Horse in a Recent Photo

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  455 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Country superstar Miranda Lambert shared an adorable photo of herself, her husband, and their horse on social media. However, some fans believed that her horse outshines the singer.

In the snap posted to Instagram on November 20, Miranda Lambert can be seen posing next to her black horse. Her husband, New York City Police Department Officer Brendan McLoughlin, can also be seen in the picture.

In the caption, Lambert, who is also an incredible songwriter, included a quote from Gina Carey: "A strong woman looks a challenge in the eye and gives it a wink."

Miranda Lambert at the Academy of Country Music Awards on April 14, 2018 in Las Vegas, Nevada. | Photo: Getty Images

The award-winning singer looked stunning in her black dress and blonde hair, but other Lambert fans did not notice her natural beauty. Instead, they praised her beautiful horse more. One fan wrote:

"Can't stop looking at that horse. Sorry, Miranda."

Another fan wrote in the comments section of the singer's post that the horse is gorgeous. Someone else commented, calling the horse "amazing." As of this writing, the shot received more than 21,000 likes.

Meanwhile, Lambert, who once revealed that she struggled with her weight at times, made sure to turn heads when she arrived at the 2020 Country Music Association (CMA) Awards recently, as reported by Fox News.

In the visual, Lambert can be seen riding a horse and comes home to her husband.

Screenshot of a comment from Miranda Lambert's Instagram post. | Photo: Instagram/idyllwind

For the star-studded event, where she was up for seven CMAs this year, Lambert opted for a pink ensemble as she appeared at the Music City Center in Nashville, Tennessee. She stunned everyone in a bright pink dress.

Wearing minimal makeup, the "Bring Me Down" singer let her blonde hair loose. She completed her look with an electric pink clutch and pumps. She attended the event with her husband, McLoughlin.

Last month, Lambert premiered the new music video for her song, titled "Settling Down," which was released in 2019. The video features McLoughlin, as reported by PEOPLE.

In the visual, Lambert can be seen riding a horse and comes home to her husband. She looked pretty in a light blue dress. In one scene, the singer writes song lyrics down in a journal and watches McLoughlin fish.

In a separate scene, Lambert and McLoughlin hold hands and cuddle as they rest on a hammock. Toward the end of the video, the loved-up couple giggles together and embrace in a hug.

Lambert exchanged her wedding vows with McLoughlin in January 2019. Their big day took place in Davidson County, Tennessee. Lambert was previously married to country singer Blake Shelton from 2011 to 2015.
