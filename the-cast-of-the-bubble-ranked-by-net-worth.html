Judd Apatow and Pam Brady wrote The Bubble’s script, whose concept was filming during a pandemic, and the actors were obligated to live together in a hotel during the shoot to stay in a bubble. This concept was inspired by the movie Jurassic World: Dominion. The movie was picked up by Netflix in November 2020 with producer Apatow at the helm, who has released blockbusters over the years, including The 40-Year Old Virgin, Knocked-Up, and Trainwreck. The film began production in the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic, and the plot revolved around a similar situation in a fictional context.

The star-studded cast included Karen Gillan, Fred Armisen, James McAvoy, and Leslie Mann. Let’s look at their net worth and how much they have earned over the years.

Karen Gillan ($3 million)

The lead of the movie, Karen Gillan, plays the role of a washed-up actress named Carol Cobb, who is trying to save her career. As mentioned by The Things, the actress began her career playing supporting roles and has starred in popular movies to amass a $3 million net worth. Her most notable works include The Guardians Of The Galaxy, Jumanji: Welcome To The Jungle, and The Dual.

Fred Armisen ($8 million)

A Saturday Night Live alum, Fred Armisen is a comedian, writer, director, and producer. A man with many talents, he has made memorable appearances in television and movies throughout his career and earned an $8 million net worth. In The Bubble, he is the independent movie director hired to shoot the Cliff Beats 6 movie.

Kate McKinnon ($9 million)

Kate McKinnon has been a fixture of Saturday Night Live since 2012 and has entertained audiences with her comedic timing and impersonations. Her popularity helped her bag roles in popular movies such as Office Christmas Party, Ghostbusters, and Bombshell, which have amassed her $9 million over the years.

Daisy Ridley ($9 million)

Daisy Ridley appeared in an interesting cameo in The Bubble as Kate, a virtual fitness trainer for Pedro Pascal’s character. Star Wars fans were delighted to see the crossover as the actress is the lead in the Star Wars series while Pascal stars in The Mandalorian in the television series. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Ridley has a $9 million net worth from portraying Rey in a galaxy far, far away and has worked on other minor projects.

Pedro Pascal ($10 million)

Pedro Pascal is cast as Dieter Bravo, an actor playing the antagonist in the movie and dealing with an addiction to drugs. Pascal became a household name for winning hearts with his short but memorable performance as Oberyn Martell in the hit HBO show Game Of Thrones and the lead in Narcos. He has actively worked in movies, and television shows ever since and collected a $10 million fortune.

Keegan-Michael Key ($12 million)

Known for his comedy sketch series with Oscar-winning screenwriter Jordan Peele called Key & Peele, Keegan-Michael Key has made appearances in several movies to earn a $12 million net worth. In The Bubble, he plays the actor Sean Knox who is trying to promote himself off the camera as a wellness guru.

James McAvoy ($20 million)

James McAvoy has made a hefty net worth starring in five X-Men movies as Professor X, which remains his most popular role to date. As noted by Screen Rant, he makes a cameo in the movie playing himself and successfully tackles Gillan’s character at the end of the movie. His appearance and mention of the X-Men series have fans speculating about his return as Professor X.

Benedict Cumberbatch ($40 million)

Benedict Cumberbatch made a cameo in The Bubble, appearing as himself in a drug-induced hallucination on the face of Karen Gillan’s character. Cumberbatch has starred in several notable movies, including The Power Of The Dog and The Imitation Game. He gained worldwide recognition for starring in Doctor Strange and made over $13 million for his standalone movies. He has amassed a $40 million fortune through his appearances in movies and television shows.

David Duchovny ($80 million)

David Duchovny has been a household name for many years due to his performance in the award-winning shows The X-Files and Californication. He was paid $225,000 per episode for the latter show and has collected an $80 million net worth through his projects. He portrays the character of Dustin Mulray in The Bubble and is the lead of the Cliff Beasts 6 movie.

Leslie Mann ($150 million)

Leslie Mann is the wife and frequent muse of Judd Apatow, the movie's creator. The couple has collaborated on several projects, including Knocked-Up, and This Is 40, as stated by The Richest. Mann portrays an actress in the movie, who is a fan-favorite character in the Cliff Beats series and the love interest of Duchovny’s character Dustin.

Other notable actors in the movie include Judd Apatow, Leslie Mann’s daughter Iris Apatow, and John Cena, to name a few. The Bubble has been streaming on Netflix since April 01, 2022.
