Overall Meaning

The lyrics of David Santos's song No Eres La Oficial convey a sense of desperation and longing for someone who is not the official partner. The opening lines immediately establish that the singer is unable to resist the temptation to have the person he desires once more. He expresses a lack of control and a desire to kidnap her. The singer acknowledges that the object of his affection is not the "official," indicating that he is not in a relationship with her, and it is not clear if he is in any relationship at all. He goes on to state that he knows his love life is not going well but implies that this person has also had the same experience. So, he flirts with her, hoping she will respond positively, and they can have a moment together away from the crowd. The singer declares his love for her and hopes that she will be his.
