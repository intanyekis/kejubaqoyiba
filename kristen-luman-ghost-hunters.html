Wiki/Bio Facts

Interest in the paranormal

Kristen graduated with a BA in Psychology from Portland State University. For the most part, she focused her studies on Paranormal Psychology and Human Development. During her college years, Kristen while preparing to go on to graduate school was introduced to hypnotherapy. Recalling her early years, Kristen revealed,

‘I should look into this, if I’m going to help people, I should learn all the possible tools. I met with a hypnotherapist myself and was astounded by the simplicity and the effectiveness of the process.’

Since her early encounter with the hypnosis, Kristen is obsessed with unlocking the power of the mind.

‘I decided after only one visit that this is what I should be doing if I truly wanted to make a positive difference in people’s lives. We use about 88% of our subconscious mind compared to 12% of our conscious mind. So, it made more sense to me that since hypnotherapy works directly with the subconscious mind this is how I should be helping people.’

Life after college

Following her graduating, Kristen enrolled at the Hypnosis Motivation Institute and became a certified hypnotherapist. A part American Hypnosis Association, Kristen also received certifications in Pain Management and Energy Healing. She also holds a diploma in Clinical Hypnotherapy with Honors from the Hypnosis Motivation Institute. She is also a Certified Specialist in Hypnotherapy and Weight Loss and also has Advanced Training in NLP. Even though Kirsten recognized for her skills, the reality star has never stopped learning. She continued her education with the American Hypnosis Education and received certifications in Advanced Child Hypnosis, Hypnosis in History, and is also a Therapeutic Imagery Facilitator. Kristen also conducts regular classes in Guided Imagery Meditation at Unplug Meditation.

Reality TV

Kristen’s first-ever appearance in reality TV came in 2013 when she starred in Ghost Mine. The series featured the happenings of the Crescent Mine which is believed to be haunted. A group of miners and ghost hunters dived deep within the mine to search for gold while also busting some ghosts. Unfortunately, the series was canceled after two seasons. Kristen bowed out from the reality TV scene after the show’s end, only to come back with Ghost Hunters. The co-lead investigator and a paranormal researcher on the team, Kristen has used her knowledge of parapsychology in investigating over 100 cases. Tasked with dealing with the witness, Kristen uses several techniques from her arsenal to calm the witness and get to the bottom of the sightings.
