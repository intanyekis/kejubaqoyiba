Who is Shirley Chen?

Shirley Chen is an American actress, writer, and designer who has acted in many screen projects. Shirley Chen is popular for featuring in Speechless, Gamer’s Guide, Parenthood, etc.

In 2023, she was seen in the role of Young Jenny (16-22 yrs old) in the comedy movie Quiz Lady along with other stars named Awkwafina, Sandra Oh, Jason Schwartzman, and Holland Taylor.

This film has got a fair response from the audience as it has ratings of 6.8 on IMDb and only 79 percent on Rotten Tomatoes.

Her upcoming screen projects Roots That Reach Toward the Sky as Kai and At Least There’s Hotpot as Mia are in the post-production stage and will be released soon.
