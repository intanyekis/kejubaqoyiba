'RHOD': What Is LeeAnne Locken's Net Worth?

Of all the cast members on the Real Housewives of Dallas, LeeAnne Locken gets into arguments a lot and that is what she is mostly known for. This entry in the Bravo franchise has some juicy episodes that fans ranked on IMDb and fans have gotten to know LeeAnne over four seasons.

Everyone can check out the fifth season in January 2021, which is super exciting news.

Everyone is curious about how much money LeeAnne Locken has, especially since she lives a very lavish life in Dallas. Let's take a look at her net worth.

$2.5 Million Net Worth

Many Real Housewives stars have a lot of money in the bank, like Garcelle Beauvais and her $8 million net worth.

What about LeeAnne?

LeeAnne Locken has a net worth of $2.5 million.

According to The Cinemaholic, she has been on the TV series Chase, GCB, and Home Improvement. She lived in L.A. where she acted and modeled, so it sounds like she made some money there.

The website also mentions Infinity Dress, which is the clothing business that LeeAnne started.

According to the reality star's IMDb page, she produced the 2012 reality series Luxury Dallas and also two episodes of Beautiful You HDTV in 2013. It seems like that would have made her some money as well. LeeAnne also hosted the reality series Luxury Dallas, along with Beautiful You HDTV and Big Rich Texas.

LeeAnne's Story

It's interesting to hear LeeAnne's story: she worked at carnivals as a child and that was a big part of her young life. According to Vice.com, she helped out with a game involving duck ponds when she was only three years old, and worked with a balloon game when she was older. This was because her mom's new husband worked at carnivals.

LeeAnne told Vice that she was paid quite a lot to work carnivals and she shared, "That was my job. I came home [from first grade] and was like, OK, I learned how to make change, so now I don't have to go back to school. I thought that was what they sent me to school for."

LeeAnne was also a big part of the pageant world. According to Romper.com, she made the top 6 place in Miss America in 1989. She is very involved with charity, which she has talked about on the reality show, and that all started during this time in her life.

According to Bravo TV, LeeAnne Locken said in February 2020 that she didn't want to be on the show anymore. She told People, "The last four years have been a trolley-slapping good time but after much consideration, I have made the personal decision to step away from RHOD." She mentioned her wedding as a highlight and she said that she wants to focus on other things in her life. LeeAnne explained, "I am looking forward to stepping away from the cameras and spending quality time with my husband and friends, traveling, but most importantly, getting back to philanthropy, which was my main reason for joining the show.”
