Full Details On YouTubes New Animated Series Dallas &amp; Robo feat. John Cena; Trailer &amp; Scree

 Posted on June 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  315 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

WWE has released the following to WrestleZone:

YouTube Red has released the trailer for upcoming original series “Dallas & Robo,” starring Kat Dennings and John Cena, 

“Dallas & Robo” is produced by ShadowMachine, the award-winning production company behind “Bojack Horseman.” The series stars Kat Dennings and John Cena, and guest voices include Jane Lynch, Giancarlo Esposito, Taran Killam, Clancy Brown, Nat Faxon, Dana Snyder, Bree Williamson and Milana Vayntrub. The series was created by Mike Roberts (“BoJack Horseman,” “Final Space”).

The eight-episode buddy comedy follows sassy space trucker Dallas (Kat Dennings) and cowboy-hat-wearing-AI robot Robo (John Cena) as they try and make a few dollars in the seedy world of interplanetary big-rigging, while navigating their way through cannibal bikers, rival space truckers and vending machine burritos.

The eight-episode buddy comedy will launch on May 30th on the WWE YouTube channel, with all episodes available to binge at that time

YouTube Red is a subscription that gives you access to YouTube Red Original movies and series, a premium music service and an uninterrupted experience across YouTube. YouTube Red is currently available in the United States, Australia, New Zealand, Mexico and Korea.

Best known for its bold storytelling, world-building and comedy-raft, ShadowMachine is a trusted award-winning production house delivering the full spectrum of animation formats. Company founders, Alex Bulkley and Corey Campodonico, continue to challenge audiences and push the boundaries of art-in-motion.
