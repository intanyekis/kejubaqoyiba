What Amanda Bynes Looks Like Now

 Posted on May 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  368 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Amanda Bynes made a rare public appearance, but passerby might not have even realized it was her considering she looks so different!

Bynes, who was largely popular in the 90's thanks to the Nickelodeon television shows All That and The Amanda Show, has hardly seen in public the past few years. After numerous social media faux pas, one of which was a very suggestive comment directed towards rapper Drake, Bynes shied away from the limelight. Now, she is hardly recognizable.

Bynes was spotted on Sept. 30, 2017, while grocery shopping at a Ralphs located in Los Angeles.  Bynes' sanity was called into question numerous times following her social media outbursts, but she does appear to be healthy, although different.  She was spotted sporting a tan cardigan, white T-shirt, black pants, a small Chanel purse, and over-sized clear frames.

A report from Page Six revealed that after a while, more and more people began to recognize her, and she got tired of all of the attention. She then tried to shield her face from all of the oncoming photographs.

Bynes should get used to the attention, however, since she had previously announced that she missed acting and would re-enter the limelight and continue her acting career.

“I do miss acting and I have something surprising to tell you: I’m going to start acting again,” she told Good Morning America.  “I want to do TV. Maybe a few guest spots on some shows that I’m a fan of and maybe another TV show where I’m the star of it.”

Since it has been a long time since we have seen anything new from Bynes, we wonder if she will ever be able to regain the immense popularity that she once held in the 90's. Even those who were fans of her work on All That and The Amanda Show might have a hard time recognizing her with her new appearance.

Still, it is nice to see her looking healthy, sober, and not in jail, considering she is on probation after a DUI and two alleged hit-and-runs. What do you think about Amanda Bynes' new look?
