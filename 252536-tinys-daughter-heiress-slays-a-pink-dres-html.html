Tiny's Daughter Heiress Slays in a Pink Dress as She Enjoys the Perfect Date with Her Dad TI

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  472 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Four-year-old Heiress Diana Harris went out for a fancy date with someone special on Valentine's Day. The toddler's mother, R&B singer Tiny Harris, is very familiar with the person her daughter dated.

Tiny Harris, a former Xscape vocalist, recently allowed her daughter, Heiress Diana, to go on a date. The man the little girl had dated was someone really special to her.

Heiress took to Instagram to share a picture of herself on a date with her favorite person, her father, Clifford Joseph Harris Jr., famously called T.I.

Tiny Harris with her daughter Heiress Diana Harris backstage during Majic 107.5 After Dark at City Winery in Atlanta, Georgia on September 3, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

A SWEET FATHER-DAUGHTER DATE

In the adorable snap, Heiress beautifully braided her hair with beads on the ends. She completed her look with a pink dress and a cute laugh while looking at her perfect date. She wrote in her post:

"My Valentine ?"

Beside her is T.I., who looked dapper in a light gray suit and dotted necktie. The 40-year-old Atlanta-based rapper looked delighted at his charming little girl.

The father-daughter duo sat on a table with heart-printed plates and sliced fruits in the middle. The words "MY VALENTINE" and a big pink heart topped the picture.

Heiress had an inexpensive date with the "Live Your Life" hitmaker. Because of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic and children being discouraged from going out, the occasion only took place at home.

Aside from sharing a table with T.I., Heiress took pictures of them together, which was shown in her YouTube account, "Heiress Does It All." The charming girl told T.I., "Daddy, I love you."

THE COUPLE'S DRAMATIC DAUGHTER

Heiress' video was the latest one she had. The little girl is known for her hilarious TikTok and Instagram clips, which have become a source of happiness for social media fans.

Tiny and T.I. are proud to have Heiress as their daughter, who inherited their showmanship. Recently, the doting mother shared an old video of her being dramatic on camera.

The little girl is the seventh child in the couple's blended family.

A LITTLE FASHIONISTA

Besides showing off her acting skills, Heiress also showed her fashion prowess. She posed twinning outfits with her parents, from winter coats to camouflage sweats.

Heiress also recently explored a new responsibility as an aunt to her sister Zonnique Pullins' seven-week-old newborn. Pullins said that her little sister was the "better aunt" than her other siblings.

Tiny and T.I., who tied the knot in July 2010 in Miami Beach, Florida, welcomed Heiress in March 2016. The little girl is the seventh child in the couple's blended family.

Aside from Heiress and Pullins, the "About the Money" rapper and Harris are co-parenting their other children - Deyjah Amani, Domani, King C'Andre, Major Philant, and Messiah Ya'Majesty.
