Everything We Know About Kendall Jenner's Relationship Status

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1195 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kendall Jenner is one of the Kardashian Jenner sisters. She and her sisters have a reality show that follows the lives of the girls and other close family members. Kendall is the second last-born and works mostly as a model. In 2018, Forbes named her the highest-paid model. Out of the Kardashian sisters, she is the only one without a baby.

The model has always been super silent about the men she is dating probably due to the attention that her sisters’ relationships have generated. The model has dated a number of celebrities over the years, among the musicians and basketball players. Her latest fling was with basketball player, Ben Simmons. Owing to their busy schedules the couple has continued to have an off and on relationship.

During those breaks, Kendall has been associated with other men stirring up rumors that she is moving on from Ben. Here is a look at what is going on with her current relationship.

15 Kendall And Fai Dress Up For Halloween

Come Halloween of 2018, Kendall had moved on. This time she was hanging out with Fai Khadra. The two spent the holiday together dressed as Fembot and Austin Powers. Kendall posted a photo of the two of them on her IG page. In addition to the photo, Kendall also put up an interesting caption directed at Fai, which elle.com has put up on their website.

14 Kendall Makes Out With Anwar Hadid

Rumors that Kendall was dating basketball player Ben Simmons started surfacing around mid-2018. However, at around September 2018, Cosmopolitan Magazine reports that Kendall was seen cozying up to Anwar Hadid. Although there are no photos to support this claim, the same source confirms that Kendall was with the Hadids on the supposed night.

13 Kendall Confirmed That She was Dating Ben

In early 2019, Kendall made an appearance on the Ellen DeGeneres Show. Nbcsports.com says the show host put a photo of the Kendall watching basketball player Ben Simmons on the court on the big screen and directly asked her how long she had been dating him. She responded by saying that they have been seeing each other for a bit.

12 She Remains Uncertain About Her Relationship

During one of her breaks from Ben, Kendall made a statement explaining why she was so super secretive about her love life. The model is very young and long-term relationships at her age are not certain, she said. It was at this time that people began speculating that things were not okay between the two, dailymail.co.uk reports.

11 Kendall Is Spotted At A Restaurant With Fai

Around Oct 2019, elitedaily.com reports that Kendall and Fai started hanging out in public again. The two shared a meal at a restaurant. Fai and Kendall will spend a considerable amount of time together as we are about to see so it is only natural for people to assume that there is something going on between the two.

10 The Beautiful Model Attends A Wedding With Fai

Kendall and Fai continued hanging out together, which only sparked more dating rumors. One of the instances that lead people to believe that Kendall and Fai were dating is when the two appeared as each others' dates at a wedding. Elitedaily.com confirms that the pair attended Justin Bieber and Hailey Baldwin’s wedding together.

9 Come To Think Of It, The Two Were At Coachella Together

Kendal and Fai refused to confirm or deny the allegations that they were romantically involved. They continued with their usual business leaving people guessing whether the two were an item or not. In April 2019, Life & Style Mag reports that the duo was together at Coachella and appeared very much like a couple.

8 The Pair Also Went To London Together

Whether Kendall and Fai were dating or not, we must admit that the pair looks good together especially that time when they wore matching outfits to Wimbledon’s basketball game in London. Pagesix.com confirms that the two both wore blue shirts and white bottoms. They were also in sneakers and accessorized their looks with shades.

7 Kendall Plays With The Idea Of Starting A Family With Fai

People are still convinced that there is something going on between Fai and Kendall. Kendall is of course not too thrilled about people being all up in her business so she goes to Instagram and asks her fans to vote whether she should have a baby with Fai, elle.com reports. As usual, her sisters were part of the craze.

6 But Fai Is Linked To Jordyn Woods

Jordyn Woods who was Kylie Kardashian’s best friend made headlines when she hooked up with Khloe Kardashian’s man Tristan Thompson. As if that was not enough drama, she was close to Fai Khadra. Cheatsheet.com confirms that Jordyn and Fai were together in New York in 2017 and there is a photo of the two holding hands.

5 He Also Had That Thing With Kourtney Kardashian

In 2018, Fai was also rumored to be involved with Kourtney Kardashian. According to Cosmopolitan Magazine, the two hang out immediately after Kourtney broke up with her French boyfriend Younes Bendjima. Interestingly, Younes bears a striking resemblance to Fai. Given Fai’s history, we are beginning to doubt that Kendall would want to be involved romantically with Fai.

4 Confirmed, Fai And Kendall Are Not Dating, They're Just Friends

At some point, Kendall had to dismiss rumors that she was dating Fai. According to elle.com, the model chose the day of Bieber’s wedding to point out that Fai and her were nothing more than two friends accompanying each other to an event. She made it clear that Fai was just her date for the day and that there was nothing going on between the two.

3 People Speculate About Kendall’s Type In Men

Kendall’s dating history as seen on stylecaster.com does not reveal that she has a certain type of men that she dates. She has supposedly been involved with musicians Justin Bieber and Harry Styles. Kendall was also rumored to be involved with Formula 1 driver, Lewis Hamilton. The model has also been linked to a few basketball players among them Ben Simmons.

2 Kendal Spends New Years With Ben

In December 2019, Kendall attended a basketball game that Ben was playing. TMZ also confirms that the Kendall ushered New Year with Ben. According to the source, cameras caught the pair heading to Attico, a rooftop restaurant in New York, which Ben had rented for the night to celebrate the New Year.

1 She Was Recently Seen With Ben After The SuperBowl

Ben and Kendall were also together after this year’s Superbowl, which took place in Miami. According to thesun.co.uk, the two were relaxing by a pool in a luxury hotel in Miami. Ben was also in Miami for work. His team, the Philadelphia 76ers played the Miami Heat later that day.
