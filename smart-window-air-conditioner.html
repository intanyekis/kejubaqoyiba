    • What is a Smart Window Air Conditioner?

    The smart air conditioner is an air conditioner unit that connects to the other smart devices in your home to better control your home’s comfort level. It looks similar to standard room air conditioners.

    Typically, the smart window air conditioner is mounted to windows or walls, instead of being centrally located in a home. Much like other smart home technology, a smart air conditioner can be programmed to cool on your terms. You can turn it on and off remotely.

    The core requirement for a smart window air conditioner is the ability to connect to Wi-Fi. So, you can control your air conditioner remotely using your mobile device whether you are at home or not.

    What makes Window Air Conditioner Smart? How does it work?

    Smart window air conditioner typically doesn’t need a remote for functionality. It generates cold air that circulates throughout the rooms in your home when you give it the command using a Wi-Fi connection to your smartphone or device or by voice.

    You can turn on a smart window air conditioner by using your smartphone, tablet, or any smart device. Some smart window air conditioner units can be connected to home assistants such as Alexa or Google for easy voice activation.
