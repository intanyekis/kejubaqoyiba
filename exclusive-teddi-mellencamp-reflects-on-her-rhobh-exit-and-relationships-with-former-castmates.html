Exclusive: Teddi Mellencamp Reflects On Her 'RHOBH' Exit And Relationships With Former Castmates

 Posted on July 5, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  841 words  |  Larita Shotwell

While fans await the newest season of Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, they're probably wondering what Teddi Mellencamp thinks of her departure from the show.

The ALL IN owner already announced that she plans to return to our television screens in the near future. When it comes to any leftover Bravo drama, she exclusively told TheThings where she stands with the Beverly Hills women.

Still Friends With Kyle

Friendship with Kyle Richards: Where do you stand today? How did it feel to make a genuine friendship on TV?

“I love Kyle! I didn’t go into Housewives expecting to make a life-long bestie but it was definitely a plus. We get along so well and so do our families.”

The rumors regarding whether or not her friendship with Richards post-filming are clearly false. The ‘Wizard of Oz’ curtain was pulled back only to reveal that everything was exactly as it seemed in the Hollywood Hills field of California poppies.

We had to ask if this genuine tie to the OG Beverly Hills cast member will mean a reappearance as a ‘friend of’ role on RHOBH, to which she simply teased, “Maybe, maybe not.”

When do you think there was a turning point in your friendship with Dorit? You started your time on the show as getting along really well, then last season she makes odd statements about your friendship with Kyle.

“Dorit and I just communicate differently and it creates some issues. But we’re able to always work past them.”

Mellencamp also noted that their children get along super well, and she recently saw Dorit just the other day. The thing about friends working out tensions on TV, which she delved into with no emotional wall to break down, is that every little tone of annoyance is made public.

Have we ever gotten upset over a friend saying an off-comment during a girl’s night out? Yes of course! Those aren’t captured in video footage, however, to sit down and rehash nine months later.

No, he didn’t, but everything happens for a reason. I’m excited for what’s to come next!"In a matter of months in quarantine, she moved into a beautiful Encino home and gave birth to her daughter Dove. She's already manifested what's to come next, and that's not even including her goals vision board that she keeps under wraps. Reflecting on 'RHOBH'

You were criticized for not speaking up early on in RHOBH, then criticized when you took part in the drama. How frustrating was that? Does it make you feel like you couldn’t win with the audience? "You know, I went into things just reacting authentically. You can’t gauge how the audience will react. So it is what it is. But those tweets can be brutal!"While keyboard warriors way feel a vindictive kind of satisfaction after posting a cruel tweet, Mellencamp has fostered a mindset that blocks out that negativity.
