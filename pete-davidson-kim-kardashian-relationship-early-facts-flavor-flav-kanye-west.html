Early Details From Pete Davidson And Kim Kardashian's Relationship, Including Flavor Flav's Role

Throughout 2021, a lot of unusual things happened, and one of those was the connection and relationship between Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson. Ever since the "Aladdin and Jasmine" Disney sketch on Kim's Saturday Night Live hosting gig, the pair began spending a lot of time together, but never actually gave fans information about their relationship. The whole thing became difficult for fans to deal with, because the pairing came as a surprise to everyone. But now, it's hard to deny how intriguing it is seeing the comedian and the reality star spend time together and form a closer bond.Something that may be considered by many as even more shocking is that the first official picture of Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson together was posted by famous rapper Flavor Flav. 8 Kim Kardashian And Pete Davidson's Bond May Have Started With A Simple Kiss

The exact time Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson began hanging out is a blur to most, however, many speculations have pointed towards the Disney sketch scene when the pair held hands and locked lips on a magic carpet. In fact, only a few weeks after that kiss, fans spotted the pair holding hands on a roller coaster, while on what appeared to have been a double date with Travis Barker and Kourtney Kardashian at Buena Park, California. Shortly after news got out, a source told People, "They hang in the same circles so they will be together from time to time. It's just friends hanging out."

7 Kim And Pete's Relationship Went From 'Friends Hang Out' To Private Dinner Dates

At this point, it was either our minds were playing tricks on us or something was actually going on between Pete Davidson and Kim Kardashian. Following the pair being spotted holding hands, they were later reported to have been on a private dinner date at an Italian restaurant in New York's Staten Island.

A source close to the pair came forward to Page Six to share that Pete Davidson had a dinner reservation setup privately on the rooftop, and also added that they didn't have any security with them, to reduce the chances of being spotted. Around this time, a report by E! News went out sharing what it is Kim may have seen in Pete. It read, "Pete is a very chill and casual guy, and she likes that. They have plans to see each other again while Kim is in New York City. Whatever is going on between them is exciting to her."

6 Reality Started To Ease In

Just like with her sister, Kourtney Kardashian, and her man, Travis Baker, Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson decided to change the narrative surrounding them by subtly making things official. They did this with some very light PDA, as they were seen holding hands while walking down a street. This basically set the record straight for many onlookers and made people aware that something was going on between Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson.

5 Pete Davidson’s Birthday Came With A Huge Surprise

Only a day after Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson were spotted in town holding hands, the pair took things to a new level, as the first official picture of them together was posted. The day, December 15th, also happened to be Pete Davidson’s birthday.

It was later revealed that the pair weren’t alone for his birthday celebration, as they had a few family members join, and Kim also had a surprise guest just for Pete. According to Kim K., Pete had been talking about how much he liked rapper Flavor Flav and to show Pete how much he meant to her, she invited the rapped over for his birthday.

4 Flavor Flav, The Birthday Present

While in an interview with ET, Flavor Flav, the talented musician and hype man, confirmed that he was indeed at Pete’s birthday for a special reason. In his words, “When it comes down to Pete Davidson and Kim Kardashian, first of all, to me, that is family, you know what I'm saying. And the whole night I was so honored to be in their presence. One thing that I could say is Pete Davidson loves himself some Flavor Flav. Everything he does is, 'Flavor Flav this, Flavor Flav that, Flavor Flav this, Flavor Flav that.' So, Kim said, 'OK, well, for your birthday, you are going to have Flavor Flav.' She sent for me and that's how I ended up there.”

Flavor Flav also added that he was touched by how much love Pete Davidson had for him and to show this, he did something he’d never done before in his entire life. The star said “I was the birthday gift. And check this out, being that that man had so much love for me, I did something for the first time ever in my life. I took my clock from around my neck and I gave it to him, and I never gave a clock to nobody.”

3 Flavor Flav's Opinion On Kim Kardashian And Pete Davidson's Relationship

Speaking on his time spent with the King of Staten Island star, Flavor Flav was asked for his opinion on what was going on between Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson based on his first-hand experience with the pair.

He said, “When it comes down to Pete Davidson and Kim Kardashian's relationship, only them two know it best. They just invited me into the circle, but I really don't know the full [story]. And plus besides, that's Kim and Pete, man. I would rather them tell about their situation than me. But I'm grateful to them that they gave me the opportunity to be the first to publicize the picture with both of them together.”

2 Kim Kardashian And Pete Davidson Began The New Year With A 'Baecation'

It's almost like the fun never ends with this pair, as they were repeatedly seen together on what seemed like purposely planned dates. On January 5, 2022, Kim and Pete were spotted together boarding a boat in the Bahamas.

1 Pete Davidson Came Into Kim Kardashian's Life At The Perfect Time

While there have been no public confirmation or outright denial of the pair being in a full-blown relationship, it's pretty obvious that Kim Kardashian is enjoying spending time with Pete Davidson, and even more so because she has been in a messy split from rapper Kanye West. In fact, an insider recently spoke to People confirming this notion saying, "The end of her marriage was a very dark time for her and Pete has been the best antidote."
