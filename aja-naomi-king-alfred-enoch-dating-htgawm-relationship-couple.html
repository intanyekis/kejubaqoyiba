How To Get Away With Murder fans were absolutely buzzing over Aja Naomi King‘s March 11 Instagram! In the pic, the gorgeous actress is joined by her parents and co-star, Alfred Enoch, and the caption certainly suggests that the young stars are more than just friends.

“Had the best time in Paris and London,” she wrote. “Another great adventure with the people I love most in the world!!” Hmm…you don’t just take anybody on vacation with your parents, right?! Naturally, the Internet began blowing up with with speculation that these two, who play law students Michaela and Wes on the hit ABC show, could be dating IRL.

Don’t expect Aja to spill about this one, though. “I would like to clear up nothing,” she told E! News, while giggling and smiling, on March 15. “People could think what they want and I’m going to let them. He’s a beautiful man and I think I’m quite a lovely young lady.” She added that her parents “love Alfie,” but wouldn’t give a straight answer as to whether or not she’s in a relationship with her co-star.
